1 Introduction

Welcome to CampusSublet. CampusSublet ("CampusSublet," "we," "our," or "us") operates a mobile application designed to help college students find and post off-campus housing subleases near their universities. We are committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of our users.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, share, and protect information when you use the CampusSublet mobile application (the "App") or any related services we provide (collectively, the "Services"). By creating an account or using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Important Disclaimer: CampusSublet is a marketplace platform only. We do not own, manage, lease, operate, or control any properties listed on the App. We do not verify, endorse, or guarantee the accuracy of any listings, the identity of any users, or the safety or legality of any housing arrangement. All transactions and agreements are solely between the users involved. Users are encouraged to exercise caution and conduct their own due diligence before entering into any sublease agreement.

2 Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information when you use CampusSublet:

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Name: Your first and last name provided during account registration.
  • Email Address: Used to create and authenticate your account and for communications.
  • University or School Affiliation: The name of the college or university you attend or are affiliated with.
  • Profile Information: Any optional profile details you choose to add, such as a profile photo or bio.
  • Listing Information: Details you provide when creating a sublease listing, including property address or general location, rental price, lease dates, property description, and photographs of the property.
  • Messages: The content of in-app messages you send to and receive from other users.
  • Saved Listings: Information about listings you save or mark as favorites.
  • Reports: Information you submit when reporting a user or listing for policy violations or suspected fraudulent activity.

2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

When you use the App, we may automatically collect certain technical information, including:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
  • IP Address: Your internet protocol (IP) address, which may be used to infer general geographic location.
  • Usage Analytics: Information about how you use the App, including pages or features accessed, time spent in the App, search queries, and interaction patterns.
  • App Store Information: Basic information provided by the Apple App Store or Google Play Store in connection with your download or purchase of the App.

3 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Account Creation and Management: To create and maintain your user account, verify your identity, and provide you with access to the Services.
  • Platform Functionality: To allow you to create, browse, save, and manage sublease listings, and to facilitate messaging between users.
  • Communications: To send you transactional emails, including account verification, password resets, and important notices about the Services.
  • Safety and Trust: To review reports of users or listings, investigate potential violations of our Terms of Service, and take appropriate action to maintain platform integrity.
  • Improvement of Services: To analyze usage patterns and user feedback in order to improve the App's functionality, user experience, and features.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal obligations, and to respond to lawful requests from governmental or regulatory authorities.
  • Fraud Prevention: To detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent activity, unauthorized access, and other misuse of the Services.

4 User Accounts and Authentication

To access most features of CampusSublet, you must create an account. When you register, you provide us with your name, email address, and university affiliation. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials and for all activities that occur under your account.

We use your email address as the primary identifier for your account and for authentication purposes. You should use a strong, unique password and notify us immediately at support@campussublet.com if you suspect unauthorized access to your account.

By creating an account, you represent that you are at least 13 years of age and, if you are under 18 years of age, that you have obtained the consent of a parent or legal guardian to use the Services. We reserve the right to terminate accounts that we have reason to believe were created in violation of these requirements.

5 User-Generated Content (Listings, Photos, and Data)

CampusSublet allows users to create and publish sublease listings, which may include property addresses or general locations, rental prices, lease dates, descriptions, and photographs. This content is visible to other users of the App and may be searchable within the platform.

Important: By submitting a listing, you represent and warrant that you have the right to sublease the property, that the information you provide is accurate and not misleading, and that any photographs you upload are owned by you or you have the necessary rights to use and share them.

CampusSublet does not verify listing accuracy, property ownership, sublease eligibility, or the identity of any listing poster. We expressly disclaim any responsibility for the accuracy, completeness, or legality of user-submitted listings. Users rely on listings at their own risk.

You retain ownership of any content you submit to the App. By submitting content, you grant CampusSublet a non-exclusive, royalty-free, worldwide license to use, display, reproduce, and distribute that content solely for the purpose of operating and improving the Services.

6 Messaging and Communications Between Users

CampusSublet provides an in-app messaging feature that allows users to communicate directly with one another regarding listings. The content of these messages is stored on our servers to enable message delivery and to maintain your message history within the App.

While we do not routinely monitor the content of private messages, we reserve the right to review message content in connection with reported violations of our Terms of Service, safety investigations, fraud prevention efforts, or as required by law.

Users should exercise caution when sharing personal information through the in-app messaging system. CampusSublet is not responsible for the conduct of any user or for any information shared between users through the messaging feature.

Do not share sensitive personal information such as financial account numbers, Social Security numbers, or passwords through the in-app messaging system.

Your information is stored on secure servers maintained by CampusSublet or our third-party infrastructure providers. We implement technical, administrative, and organizational security measures designed to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Despite our efforts, no security system is impenetrable, and we cannot guarantee that your information is completely secure. Unauthorized entry or use, hardware or software failure, and other factors outside our control may compromise the security of user information at any time.

In the event of a data breach that is likely to result in a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities as required by applicable law.

  • Access: You may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You may update or correct inaccurate information directly through the App's account settings or by contacting us.
  • Deletion: You may request deletion of your account and associated personal information. Please note that some information may be retained as required by law or for legitimate business purposes, such as fraud prevention or dispute resolution.

11 Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the Services. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within a reasonable time, except where we are required to retain it for legal, regulatory, or legitimate business purposes.

Specifically, we may retain certain information after account deletion for the following purposes: complying with our legal obligations, resolving disputes, enforcing our agreements, preventing fraud, and maintaining security. Usage analytics data may be retained in anonymized or aggregated form indefinitely.

Listing information and messages associated with a deleted account will be removed from the App but may remain in backup systems for a limited period before being fully purged.
