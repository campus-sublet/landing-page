4 Listings

When you post a listing on CampusSublet, you are responsible for making sure everything in it is accurate and up to date. Listings must be for real, FSU-area properties that you have the right to sublease or transfer.

  • All listing details — rent, dates, location, unit type — must be truthful and complete.
  • Do not post duplicate listings for the same unit.
  • Once a sublease or room is no longer available, mark the listing as Taken promptly so other students are not misled.
  • Listings for properties outside the FSU area, or for arrangements you are not actually offering, will be removed.

CampusSublet does not independently verify listing details. Always confirm all information directly with the poster before making any housing decisions, and see a unit in person before paying anything.

5 Messaging

The in-app messaging feature exists to help students connect about housing — nothing else. Use it to ask questions about listings, coordinate viewings, and discuss lease details.

  • Messaging may only be used for housing-related communication.
  • Harassment, threats, spam, or any other abusive behavior sent through messages will result in immediate account suspension.
  • Do not share sensitive personal information (financial account numbers, passwords, etc.) through the messaging system.

CampusSublet stores messages to enable delivery and maintain your conversation history. We do not routinely read private messages, but reserve the right to review them in connection with a reported violation or safety investigation. See our Privacy Policy for details.

6 Blocking and Reporting

Your safety on CampusSublet matters. You have the following tools available at all times:

  • Report a listing — flag any listing you believe is fake, inaccurate, or violates these Terms.
  • Report a user — flag any user for harassment, abusive behavior, or any other violation.
  • Block a user — blocking a user prevents them from messaging you, viewing your profile, and hides their content from your feed. Blocking is immediate and does not notify the other user.

All reports are reviewed within 24 hours. CampusSublet will remove violating content and suspend offending accounts as warranted. CampusSublet reserves the right to remove any content and suspend any account at its sole discretion, with or without prior notice.

7 Privacy

For the full picture of how we collect, store, and handle your data, please read our Privacy Policy.
